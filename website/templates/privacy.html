Seller Privacy Policy

This Seller Privacy Policy explains how Yusuf Marketplace collects, uses, stores, and protects seller information when sellers use the platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and account details
  • Store or business information provided by the seller
  • Product details, descriptions, images, and prices
  • Order and transaction information
  • Communication sent through the platform

2. How We Use Seller Information

  • To create and manage seller accounts
  • To display product listings on the marketplace
  • To process orders, payments, and withdrawals
  • To improve marketplace operations and user experience
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell seller personal information to third parties. However, seller business information, product details, store identity, and listing content may be displayed to buyers where needed for marketplace operations.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect seller data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Seller Responsibility

Sellers are responsible for keeping their account credentials secure and ensuring the information they provide remains accurate and current.

6. Data Retention

We may retain seller data as long as necessary for platform operations, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and enforcement of our agreements.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
